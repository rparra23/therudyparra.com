In Memoriam

People who shaped my path in tech, in research, and in life. I carry their lessons forward in every project I build and every community I help grow. Wherever I go in this work, they're part of it.

Dr. Steven A. Feller

Mentor · Coe College, Physics

Professor of Physics at Coe College for 39 years, Steve mentored me through my NSF-funded REU summer of 2023 in glass physics — the work that became my first two co-authored papers on lead borate XAS and sodium borate dielectric barrier discharge plasmas. Over his career he authored 150+ papers, secured $8M+ in research grants, and gave more than 200 conference presentations.

He was the kind of mentor who made you believe you belonged in the room — who put students at the heart of his lab and trusted them with real research. That's the standard I try to hold for anyone I get to mentor in turn.
