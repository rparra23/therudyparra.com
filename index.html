Hi, I'm Rudy Parra.

Electrical lab technician & embedded systems developer building clean-energy hardware.

I'm an R&D Electrical Lab Technician working across lithium-sulfur and vanadium redox flow battery systems at GridFlow, architect of the full-stack energy platform behind Pii Energy's Edge 2000, and a published researcher in solar physics and glass physics. I like building things that bridge electrons, atoms, and code. Off the bench, I serve on the Board of NM Tech Talks and direct the New Mexico chapter of Techqueria — actively shaping the ecosystem I work in. Working with me means more than a multi-tool engineer on the bench; it means tapping into a network helping build New Mexico's tech scene.
